World Travel

December 2019: Santa Cruz, California

I have been to Santa Cruz many times, but one of my favorite trips was in December of 2019. Santa Cruz is one of my favorire places because of how beautiful the beaches are. I will always try to visit at least once a year!

January 2022: Las Vegas, Nevada

In January of 2022, I travelled to Vegas for my birthday with my older sister and brother-in-law. It was my first time to Vegas and I hope to go again some day!

August 2022: Chicago, Illinois

In August of 2022, I travelled to Chicago with my sister. I really enjoyed the food and the atmosphere here. I would definitely like to travel back here in the future!

February 2023: Pebble Beach, California

In February of 2023, I travelled to Pebble Beach to watch the PGA Pebble Beach Pro-Am. I had not been to a golf tournament yet and it was very exciting to see both players I have seen on TV, as well as the celebrities that were playing in the event with them. I hope to make this a yearly trip!
